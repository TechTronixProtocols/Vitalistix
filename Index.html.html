About MERIT

MERIT is at the forefront of metabolic epilepsy research, combining cutting-edge science with nutritional interventions to help patients achieve remission. Our mission is to empower individuals with knowledge, provide evidence-based resources, and pioneer new approaches to metabolic health management.

Through rigorous research and community collaboration, we're building a future where metabolic epilepsy is manageable through targeted nutritional strategies and personalized care protocols.

Metabolic Health Assessment

Answer these questions to assess your risk factors for metabolic epilepsy triggers.

1. Do you experience fatigue or brain fog after meals?

2. Do you have sensitivity to bright lights or loud sounds?

3. Do you experience sudden mood swings or irritability?

4. Do you have difficulty maintaining consistent energy levels?

5. Do you experience unexplained muscle weakness or tremors?

6. Do you have food sensitivities or digestive issues?

7. Do you experience sleep disturbances or insomnia?

8. Do you have difficulty concentrating or focusing?

9. Do you experience headaches or migraines frequently?

10. Do you have a family history of epilepsy or metabolic disorders?
